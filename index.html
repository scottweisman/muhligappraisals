Appraisal Services

Muhlig Appraisals specializes in library, archive, and photography appraisals, assessing and valuating complex personal and professional archival materials of poets, fiction and non-fiction prose writers, musicians and composers, and professionals in law and other fields for libraries, archives, and other educational institutions, as well as private collectors.

Additional services include expert appraisals of such materials for charitable donations, as well as valuations for insurance, estates, and trusts.
